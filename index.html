About Me

A Storyteller
Behind the Lens

I am a professional photographer based in Bukidnon, dedicated to crafting images that are as timeless as the moments they capture. With an eye for light, emotion, and authentic connection, I bring a cinematic quality to every session.

From the quiet tenderness of a debut portrait to the joyful chaos of a wedding celebration, I document the full spectrum of human experience — with intention, artistry, and care.

My work blends soft, editorial aesthetics with genuine documentary storytelling, creating images my clients treasure for generations.
